HI, I'M JAYA!

True story: in 2016, Scissorbella – the Goddess, was immaculately conceived and birthed through me.

But for the first 20 years, life happened by default. My Gemini Sun gifted me the silver tongue to script a life of a PR specialist in the spotlight. I traded peace of mind for Fendi bags and world travels. I was very, VERY good at the hustle—even if it meant peddling ‘women empowerment’ in a fairness cream jar.

At 42, hairdressing chose me. It shredded the old script and became my life’s most successful love story. But every love story, no matter how grand, is tested with shit sandwiches—the stark realities.

The real question isn't ‘what do I love?’ but ‘what do I love enough to eat those sandwiches for?’

I love hair. More than I love my ego.

In other words, I’ll keep crafting precision cuts and creative colours with absurdly long shelf lives. This grit is the soul of Scissorbella. She is a quiet counter-narrative to the hyped quick-fix culture—stubbornly precise, ardently compassionate and will put your hair’s integrity before her monetary gains.

Always.

The Scissorbella Method

A quiet counter-narrative to the "quick-fix" culture

Naked Honesty

The cleverest way to do business is the most honest way. Scissorbella does not upsell, criticize your hair to push a product, or dabble with harsh chemicals. There is no upselling and no agenda. The only thing on the table is her craft. Your hair is her canvas and protecting its health, helps Jaya’s livelihood.

Deep Dive Consultation

You are a partner, not a doll head. Expect a conversation, not a lecture. To arrive at the best suited cut and/or colour, we balance your bone structure, face shape, hair texture, density with your lifestyle non-negotiables, bad habits, hair’s health and history. We decide together. You own the result.

The Slow Tempo

We aren’t aiming for "instant." We are aiming for enduring. Slow is the real fast. A mindful slow colour fades gracefully and fiercely protects the hair health. A structural cut holds its shape for months; It’s a long-term investment to ensure that every rupee you spend at Scissorbella works harder and lasts longer.

Integrity First

If a colour service threatens your hair health, Scissorbella simply won’t do it. She’s protecting her canvas and your long-term investment.

The Long Game

You won’t leave with a "one-week wonder." You’ll walk out with a structural hair colour or cut designed for a ridiculously long shelf life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does a haircut take 2-3 hours?

She focusses on renovation rather than maintenance. Time is spent understanding your hair's history, its growth patterns, and your habits. Cutting with extreme precision honours your identity.

Why are prices higher?

Scissorbella offers longevity. While a standard cut may last from 1 to 6 weeks, a Scissorbella cut may endure for at least 3 to even 12 months. It's the economical choice when calculating "cost per wear".

Will you blow-dry my hair straight?

Scissorbella’s signature is honest hair true to its natural texture. We refrain from harsh round brush blow drying. However, we offer wash & style as a separate service for special occasions.

Do you never use heat?

We use heat purely as a technical tool in two instances: Precision Check on straight cuts to identify flaws, and diffusing curly hair to see the final texture.

Do you use only natural products?

We avoid trendy greenwashing. We use eco-ethical formulations that are effective and safe, rather than fearing all "chemicals". Water is a chemical too!

Who is the "Trespassers' Club" for?

If you understand the value of Slow Luxury, then it’s for you. No expiry deadline, shareable, and you pay less to get more!
